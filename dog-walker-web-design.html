Group walks

Small groups, proper adventures, good company

Your dog joins a small, carefully matched pack for a proper hour out — sniffing, exploring and making friends along the way. Numbers stay deliberately low, so every dog gets attention, and groups are matched by age, size and energy so everyone comes home happy.

Adventure days out

An occasional day out — limited spots

Every couple of months, Cava Crew runs a one-off adventure day — a few hours in a safely enclosed field and woodland, so even dogs who usually stay on the lead get some proper off-lead time. Spaces are limited, and every dog goes home with their own snack pack.

These usually land on a Saturday and tie in with the seasons — a summer outing, the odd holiday — as an optional extra for anyone keen. The next one is pencilled in for July (date to be confirmed).
